About me

My name is Devin DeFrank. This is an attempt to create my own website to fulfill the criteria regarding the University at Buffalo's freshman seminar CSE199 and its final project. Being born and still living in the Buffalo area, I have decided to commute instead of participating in campus housing opportunities. Living at home allows me to keep a job as well as attend school full time. Currently I work in retail, with the main goal to help pay for school and other necessities of life.
